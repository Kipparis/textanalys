President Yukino is Available Now!

Hi everyone!

Thank you so much for your patience in waiting for Yukino! Now… she’s finally here!

https://store.steampowered.com/app/887530/President_Yukino/

Since we’ve released Fallen and Treasure Hunter Claire, a lot has changed for us. We went from being a relatively unknown company to one that has many wonderful fans from all over the world. And to thank you guys, we’ve also made Fallen and Treasure Hunter Claire 30% off! So please check those games out if you haven’t yet! They’re really wonderful games!

https://store.steampowered.com/app/791330/Fallen_Makina_and_the_City_of_Ruins/

https://store.steampowered.com/app/828070/Treasure_Hunter_Claire/

You can also get an additional 10% discount by purchasing our Heroine Bundle:

https://store.steampowered.com/bundle/7694/Heroine_Bundle/


One of the weaknesses that we had with just starting out was that we had no resources, and this meant that we could only release a game about once every 3 months. However, since the release of our first game, Fallen ~Makina and the City of Ruins~ in February, every purchase of our games that you’ve made to us have gone back into fixing this weakness. So we’re happy to announce that our goal will be to try to release at least 1 - 2 games every month from this point forward.

This will start with our next release, Seed of Evil, by Hasoyua:

https://store.steampowered.com/app/892760/Seed_of_Evil/

This game will be available in 2 weeks on November 2nd. It’s a really wonderful classic RPG with a lot of neat gameplay elements and fantastic art. Please add it to your wishlist if you haven’t done so yet. I hope everyone will enjoy it!

We will also be releasing Phantom Thief Celianna by Acerola during the same month on November 16th:

https://store.steampowered.com/app/907210/Phantom_Thief_Celianna/

This is a Stealth Action game that includes Acerola’s first venture into animation. I would say more about it, but it’s an Acerola game, the same circle that made Claire and Yukino… does anything else need to be said? Just wishlist it now! XD

And don’t forget our other releases slated for the rest of the year, including Slave’s Sword by Circle Poison, and Leviathan and Reign of the Succubus by TechnoBrake!

https://store.steampowered.com/app/893010/Slaves_Sword/

https://store.steampowered.com/app/897440/Leviathan_A_Survival_RPG/

https://store.steampowered.com/app/893500/Reign_of_the_Succubus/

We’ll have exact release dates for these games next month!

Anyway, a lot of time and effort went into bringing you guys another Acerola game in English and Chinese. So we hope everyone will enjoy President Yukino!

About This Game



Seed of Evil is an epic adventure to uncover the secrets of the Water City, Somonden. Follow Anna as she explores the upper and lower parts of the city to uncover the truth of the defilement plaguing its inhabitants. Enjoy this amazing story filled with mystery, drama, and intrigue. It'll be one you won't soon forget.

Make sure you check out this RPG brought to you in native English!

Features

    • Classic RPG Gameplay
    • Immersive Characters and Story
    • Explore a Post-Apocalyptic World
    • No Grinding Required
    • Gain Experience and Level Up Through Doing Good Deeds

Mature Content Description

The developers describe the content like this:

Seed of Evil features some partial nudity and some strong language.

System Requirements

    Minimum:
    • OS: Windows® 7/8/8.1/10
    • Processor: Intel Core2 Duo or better
    • Memory: 4 MB RAM
    • Graphics: DirectX 9/OpenGL 4.1 capable GPU
    • DirectX: Version 9.0
    • Storage: 1 GB available space
    • Additional Notes: 1280x768 or better Display. Lag may occur from loading menus or maps. Turn off other programs before running the game.
    Recommended:
    • OS: Windows® 7/8/8.1/10
    • Processor: 2+ GHz Processor
    • Memory: 4 GB RAM
    • Graphics: OpenGL ES 2.0 hardware driver support required for WebGL acceleration. (AMD Catalyst 10.9, nVidia 358.50)
    • Storage: 1 GB available space
    • Additional Notes: 1280x768 or better Display. Lag may occur from loading menus or maps. Turn off other programs before running the game.
