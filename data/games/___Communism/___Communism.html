About This Game

共产主义女孩 ~ ☭ Communism（￣ー￣） is a simple slide puzzle game. Move parts of the picture around to espouse a party.

It is a sliding puzzle that consists of a frame of square tiles in random order with one tile missing. The puzzle also exists in other sizes - 2x3, 3x2, 3x3, 4x3. The object of the puzzle is to place the tiles in order by making sliding moves that use the empty space.
